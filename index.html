URBAN FORESTRY

Urban forestry is the planting,protecting and managing of trees in cities inorder to create healthier and greener environment.

BENEFITS OF URBAN FORESTRY

  • Noise reduction
  • Creates employment opportunities in the community
  • It improves mental health of people in the community
  • Urban forestry boosts tourism and bussiness in the community

    • ONGOING PROJECTS IN THE COMMUNITY

  • Tree planting programmes in the community
  • Street and roadside beautification
  • Urban park rehabilitation
  • Community education and awareness about urban forestry
  • Tree maintenance and monitoring in the community
