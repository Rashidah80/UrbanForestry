Who Are We

Urban forestry Intiative is a community organization promoting sustainable green spaces within cities and towns.The intiative was founded in 2023 working hand in hand with local residents,schools and governments to restore urban ecosystems.

Our Vision

A cleaner,cooler and more livable urban environment through the power of trees.

Our Mission

To increase tree cover in urban areas,improve air quality and rise awareness about the benefits of Urban forestry

Our Goals

  • Increasing urban tree cover by 20% by 2030
  • Training communities in sustainable tree care
  • Reducing urban pollution through tree planting
